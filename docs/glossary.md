# Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Actor | A person, organisation, or service with authenticated access. |
| Participant | An actor represented in product workflows; exact roles are product-defined. |
| Trust signal | Evidence that informs risk or quality evaluation; never a sole automatic decision for high-impact actions. |
| Case | A moderation, support, fraud, or compliance workflow with an auditable lifecycle. |
| Ledger | Immutable accounting record of financial events, separate from display balances. |
| Policy | Versioned business or safety rule enforced by a service or operator process. |
