# Live Streaming

Live Streaming owns broadcasts, rooms, hosts, guests, co-hosts/duets, group sessions, chat, moderators, gifts, tips, rankings, stream health, replay, recordings, clips, captions, and moderation signals.

Stream access requires audience, age, geography, payment, and enforcement checks at join time and continuously on material policy change. Hosts and moderators have emergency stop, reporting, and escalation controls. Recording and replay are opt-in/policy-governed.
