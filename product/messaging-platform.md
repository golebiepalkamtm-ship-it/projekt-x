# Messaging Platform

Messaging owns direct and group threads, channels, voice/video sessions, media, GIFs, stickers, reactions, quotes, forwarding, recall, self-destruct policy, voice/video notes, paid messages, and premium galleries.

Recipient consent, blocks, age/jurisdiction rules, abuse controls, retention, paid-content entitlement, and report context are enforced server-side. Presence and read receipts are separately configurable privacy controls.
