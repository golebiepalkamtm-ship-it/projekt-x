# J-003: Search and Discover

The participant enters a query, selects filters, receives eligible results, and can refine or clear the search. The interface exposes loading progress, zero-result suggestions that do not broaden safety constraints, and errors that preserve entered intent.

Results identify their basis where practical: direct match, proximity/availability if enabled, or recommendation. Users can hide, report, save, or explain a result. The system does not claim a result is verified or safe beyond its actual assertion scope.
