# Social Network

Social Network owns posts, feed projections, stories, reels, reactions, comments, shares, follows, friends, groups, communities, hashtags, trends, events, calendars, invitations, and recommended people. Every interaction respects visibility, audience, block, jurisdiction, age, and safety policy.

Feed ranking is policy-filtered before AI or engagement scoring. Stories, short-form video, and shares use expiry, rights, media, and deletion contracts. Community roles are scoped capabilities with moderation/audit requirements.
