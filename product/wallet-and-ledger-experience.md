# Wallet and Ledger Experience

If an approved product introduces balances, the participant sees available, pending, and unavailable amounts with their meaning, currency, timestamp, and linked transaction references. Display balances are derived from the ledger; users cannot edit them.

Every movement has an understandable description and support path. Corrections use compensating entries. Transfers, withdrawals, promotions, and stored value require separate policy, fraud, and regulatory approval.
