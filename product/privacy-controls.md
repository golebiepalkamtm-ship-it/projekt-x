# Privacy Controls

Privacy controls are grouped by outcome: profile visibility, discoverability, contact permissions, activity disclosure, data export, and account closure. Defaults are conservative for sensitive fields and must be reviewed when a feature adds a new audience.

Each control previews who can see the data, where change propagates, and what remains visible due to legal/safety obligations. Controls are enforced in APIs, indexes, cache, notifications, and exports—not only in the client.
