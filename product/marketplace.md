# Marketplace

Marketplace owns listings, product/service catalogues, carts, promotions, auctions, bids, buy-now flow, fulfilment state, reviews, and disputes. It consumes verified seller eligibility, wallet, notification, and trust/safety contracts.

Auctions use server time, idempotent bidding, anti-sniping policy, funds/eligibility checks, and auditable outcomes. Physical fulfilment, digital fulfilment, returns, and regulated goods are separately policy-gated.
