# J-001A: Identity and Eligibility Verification

Verification is requested only when an approved policy requires it. The flow tells the participant what is checked, why, the provider/reviewer role, expected time, data retention, alternatives if any, and appeal route.

States: `not_required`, `required`, `submitted`, `in_review`, `verified`, `failed`, `expired`, `revoked`. A failure exposes a safe, actionable reason when disclosure does not enable fraud or harm. Verification status is an assertion with issuer, scope, time, and expiry—not a copy of raw evidence.
