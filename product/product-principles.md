# Product Principles

- **User agency:** explain meaningful choices and provide controls close to the consequence.
- **Trust without surveillance:** collect the least data necessary and state why it is used.
- **Safety by default:** risky actions require friction proportional to harm, not hidden policy traps.
- **Honest systems:** distinguish verified facts, user claims, and algorithmic inferences.
- **Recoverable journeys:** empty, loading, error, review, and appeal states are first-class requirements.

Features may not use dark patterns, deceptive urgency, or unexplained score-driven restrictions.
