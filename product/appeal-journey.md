# J-006: Appeal an Enforcement Decision

An eligible participant receives a decision notice, reason category, effect, duration, appeal deadline, and safe way to submit context. Appeals are reviewed by an authorised person independent from the original decision when feasible.

Outcomes are `upheld`, `modified`, `reversed`, or `not_eligible`; each creates an auditable event and applies compensating changes. The appeal process avoids exposing reporters, detection methods, or other protected people’s data.
