# Capability Map

| Capability | Outcome | Initial maturity |
|---|---|---|
| Account access | legitimate participants access their account safely | foundation |
| Profiles | participants express verified and privacy-aware information | foundation |
| Discovery | people find relevant permitted results | discovery |
| Trust & safety | reports are triaged, decided, and appealable | safety |
| Communication | participants interact under consent and policy | controlled |
| Commerce | eligible transactions are authorised and reconcilable | controlled |
| Operations | operators act with least privilege and accountability | foundation |
| Insights | owners learn without over-collecting personal data | measured |
