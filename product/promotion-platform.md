# Promotion Platform

Promotion owns campaigns, ads, sponsored profiles/posts/streams, boosts, coupons, discounts, targeting policy, budgets, pacing, reporting, and disclosure. Paid placement is visibly labelled and never bypasses safety or eligibility filters.

Targeting excludes prohibited sensitive categories and uses approved consent/purpose rules. Campaign changes, spend, delivery, and fraud controls are auditable and reconciled with the wallet/ledger.
