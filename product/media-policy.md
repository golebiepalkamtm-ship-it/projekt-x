# Media and Upload Policy

Uploads use a quarantine pipeline: client validation, malware/content safety scanning, metadata stripping policy, transcoding, access-policy assignment, and publication decision. Original files are never public by accident.

The UI states allowed formats, size, processing status, and failure reason. Private assets use signed, short-lived delivery URLs and cannot be promoted to public visibility without explicit confirmation. Removed content is retained only under documented legal or safety requirements.
