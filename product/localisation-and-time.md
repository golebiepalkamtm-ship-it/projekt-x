# Localisation, Time, and Currency

Store timestamps in UTC with source time zone where meaningful; render in the participant’s selected locale and time zone. Do not infer legal jurisdiction solely from locale. Monetary amounts use integer minor units, ISO currency codes, and an explicit rounding policy.

All user-facing policy, safety, payment, and consent text is versioned and localised by approved translators. Fallback language must not change legal or safety meaning.
