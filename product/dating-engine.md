# Dating Engine

Dating owns preferences, relationship goals, matching, swipe/like actions, super-like policy, visits, compatibility inputs, nearby mode, geofences, invisible mode, and travel mode. Consent, visibility, age eligibility, block state, and jurisdiction rules filter candidates before matching.

Location is purpose-limited and coarse by default. Compatibility and AI matching are assistive, explainable, resettable, and prohibited from inferring sensitive attributes beyond approved participant-provided preferences.
