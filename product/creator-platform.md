# Creator Platform

Creator Platform owns creator analytics, subscriptions, VIP clubs, premium galleries, paid live experiences, paid messages, digital products, merchandise, special offers, and earnings views. It consumes wallet and commerce contracts; it does not calculate balances itself.

Creator eligibility, tax/payout onboarding, content policy, revenue sharing, refunds, and chargebacks are jurisdiction- and policy-gated. Analytics are privacy-minimised and do not expose viewer identities unless an approved feature explicitly requires it.
