# Content and Safety Policy Framework

The final permitted-content policy is blocked by RFC-0001. This framework already requires published rules, understandable enforcement notices, consistent evidence handling, case ownership, time targets, appeal paths, and policy versioning.

Reports do not promise a particular outcome. Reporters receive confirmation, reference number, privacy-safe progress updates where appropriate, and emergency guidance when a situation may require local services. Operators apply policy; they must not improvise policy through private notes.
