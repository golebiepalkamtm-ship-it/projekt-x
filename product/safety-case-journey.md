# J-005: Safety Case Lifecycle

A report or automated signal creates a case with severity, queue, policy version, evidence references, and owner. The case moves through `intake`, `triage`, `investigating`, `decision_pending`, `decided`, `appeal`, and `closed`.

Decisions identify the policy basis, scope, duration, reviewer, and notice requirements. Evidence access is need-to-know and fully audited. Cases have service targets, escalation for imminent harm, and quality review for consistency.
