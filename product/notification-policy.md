# Notification Policy

Notifications are purpose-specific, preference-aware, rate-limited, and revocable except where law or account security requires delivery. Each notification has an owner, template version, channel priority, localisation strategy, urgency level, and unsubscribe behaviour.

Security, material account changes, transactions, and case outcomes require a persistent in-product record. Marketing and non-critical engagement messages default to the least intrusive channel permitted by the participant.
