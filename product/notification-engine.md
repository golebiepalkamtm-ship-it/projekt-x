# Notification Engine

Notification Engine routes email, push, SMS, in-app, realtime, webhook, Discord, Telegram, Slack, digest, reminder, campaign, and recommendation messages. Channels are enabled per purpose, consent, jurisdiction, sensitivity, provider, and audience.

Critical security and safety notices use persistent in-product records. Bulk/campaign delivery has approval, audience preview, rate limiting, suppression, unsubscribe, localisation, monitoring, and rollback.
