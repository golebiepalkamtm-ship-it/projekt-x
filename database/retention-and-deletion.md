# Retention and Deletion

Retention is set by purpose, legal obligation, operational need, and risk—not convenience. Each data category records trigger, duration, legal review need, anonymisation/deletion action, and downstream systems.

Deletion is a workflow: identify source and derived records, revoke access, remove or anonymise eligible data, issue provider deletion requests, retain narrowly scoped evidence where required, and record completion. Backups age out on a documented schedule; they are not individually edited.
