# Entity: Enforcement Action

**Owner:** Trust & Safety. An action is a scoped, time-bound policy outcome. It includes subject, capability/resource scope, action type, effective/expiry time, policy reason, case reference, decision actor, notice status, and appeal eligibility.

Enforcement consumers receive an event and maintain fast policy projections. Expiry is processed deterministically; lifting or changing an action creates a replacement record, never edits history.
