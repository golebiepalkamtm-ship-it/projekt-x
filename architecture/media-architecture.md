# Media Architecture

Media flows through upload quarantine, malware and policy scanning, metadata handling, transcoding, derivative generation, rights/visibility assignment, CDN delivery, and deletion propagation. Source assets and public derivatives have independent access policies.

Live media uses adaptive bitrate, region-aware delivery, stream health monitoring, replay/clip policy, and emergency termination controls. AI assistance may caption, blur, or flag; it cannot make irreversible moderation decisions alone.
