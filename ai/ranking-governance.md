# Ranking Governance

Ranking may order already eligible results; it cannot override visibility, safety, or policy exclusions. Signals are documented with purpose, freshness, provenance, controllability, and bias risk. Restricted data and sensitive inferences are excluded unless a specific approved policy says otherwise.

Offline metrics include relevance and safety; online metrics include reports, hides, zero-result rate, and fairness guardrails. Material ranking changes are versioned, monitored, and rollbackable.
