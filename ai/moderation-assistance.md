# Moderation Assistance

AI may prioritise queues, classify likely policy categories, or summarise permitted case material for trained reviewers. It cannot issue irreversible enforcement autonomously. Output displays uncertainty, source/version, and relevant evidence boundaries.

False positives, false negatives, language coverage, adversarial bypass, and reviewer-overreliance are tested before release. Reviewers can disagree and record why; those corrections feed evaluation, not automatic retraining.
