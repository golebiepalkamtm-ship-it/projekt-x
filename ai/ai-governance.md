# AI Governance

AI capabilities are products with an owner, intended use, excluded use, data inventory, evaluation plan, monitoring thresholds, and rollback procedure. Training or inference data must be minimised, classified, and approved for the stated purpose.

High-impact outputs are advisory unless an approved policy says otherwise. The interface must disclose meaningful AI assistance where it affects a user, and reviewers must see the evidence, confidence limitations, and policy version needed to challenge it.

No model may independently release funds, permanently restrict a participant, infer sensitive traits, or make legal/medical/financial eligibility determinations.
