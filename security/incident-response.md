# Incident Response

1. Detect and create an incident record with owner and severity.
2. Contain safely; preserve evidence and avoid destructive improvisation.
3. Assess affected systems, data, participants, and legal notification obligations.
4. Eradicate cause, recover services, and validate security before closure.
5. Communicate facts through approved owners; do not speculate.
6. Hold a blameless retrospective with corrective actions, owners, and due dates.

Emergency access is time-bound, justified, and fully audited.
