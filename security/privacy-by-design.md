# Privacy by Design Checklist

- Is each field necessary for a defined user value or obligation?
- Is the least sensitive representation sufficient?
- Are visibility, purpose, retention, export, correction, and deletion documented?
- Do logs, analytics, search, support, and providers receive only what they need?
- Can a user understand the consequence of their choice before data is shared?
- Are children, vulnerable people, and restricted audiences protected by default where applicable?

Privacy review is required before any new sensitive-data collection, profiling, cross-context correlation, or third-party sharing.
