# Threat Model: Foundation

## Assets

Accounts, credentials, verification evidence, private content, payment records, safety cases, audit trails, and platform configuration.

## Primary threats

Account takeover, enumeration, impersonation, privilege escalation, data exfiltration, abusive uploads, payment fraud, operator misuse, supply-chain compromise, and unsafe automated decisions.

## Baseline controls

Strong authentication, object-level authorization, opaque identifiers, rate limits, secure upload processing, least privilege, secret management, dependency controls, immutable audit events, anomaly monitoring, and incident response. Each feature adds a threat model proportionate to its harm potential.
