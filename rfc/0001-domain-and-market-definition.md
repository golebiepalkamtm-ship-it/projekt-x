# RFC-0001: Define ESEP’s market and policy envelope

## Decision required

Approve the target customer, jurisdictions, age and eligibility policy, permitted services/content, prohibited activities, and payment/identity-verification strategy.

## Why now

These choices shape data collection, moderation, payments, UX copy, retention, and compliance. No feature specification should imply these answers before they are approved.

## Success criteria

The result names accountable owners, legal review needs, measurable policy outcomes, and a migration plan if the market scope changes.
