# Initial Risk Register

| Risk | Impact | Control direction | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Market or legality is undefined | invalid product decisions | RFC-0001 and legal review before launch | product |
| Abuse scales faster than operations | user harm and regulatory risk | safety controls, capacity plan, escalation | safety |
| Over-collection of data | privacy and breach risk | data minimisation and retention controls | security |
| Payment/provider failure | loss of trust and reconciliation errors | ledgers, idempotency, provider fallback | commerce |
| Model error or bias | unfair or unsafe outcomes | evaluation, oversight, appeal, rollback | AI and safety |
