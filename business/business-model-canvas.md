# Business Model Canvas

The commercial model is intentionally unapproved. The platform’s value hypothesis is that verified, policy-compliant participants can discover and manage trusted interactions with clearer controls than unmoderated alternatives.

Before monetisation design, approve customer segments, value proposition, value exchange, provider obligations, fees, refunds, tax ownership, dispute model, and prohibited incentives. Revenue targets may never supersede safety, consent, privacy, or legal requirements.
