# Pricing and Fees Framework

Pricing is a policy-controlled product contract, not a UI string. An approved price has owner, audience, currency, tax treatment, fee allocation, effective period, refund/cancellation interaction, disclosure placement, and change-notice rule.

The platform must calculate amounts server-side, preserve the price version accepted by the participant, and reconcile display totals with ledger postings. Dynamic pricing requires a distinct fairness, transparency, and legal review.
