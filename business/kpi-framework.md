# KPI Framework

Metrics inform decisions; they are not product goals by themselves. Each metric has an owner, definition, source, refresh interval, access classification, interpretation limits, and counter-metrics.

| Area | Outcome metric | Guardrail |
|---|---|---|
| Activation | participants completing a permitted first-value action | verification abandonment and support burden |
| Discovery | successful relevant-result sessions | reports and zero-result rate |
| Trust | timely, consistent case resolution | appeal reversals and disparate impact |
| Reliability | completed critical workflows | latency and incident frequency |
| Commerce | reconciled authorised transactions | refunds, disputes and fraud loss |
